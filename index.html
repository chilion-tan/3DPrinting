Recycling an Unused Sink Support

Posted on: 22 Jun 2025 | Author: Chilion Tan

In my latest DIY project, I designed a simple ball joint to connect aluminum tubes to form a rectangular structure that would support an unused sink platform. This creative solution allowed me to repurpose materials that would otherwise go to waste.

As a fellow citizen of Gaia, I'm always looking for ways to reduce waste and minimize my environmental impact. This project not only prevented a perfectly good sink from becoming landfill material but also saved me money in the process.

The aluminum tube structure provides excellent stability while remaining lightweight. The ball joint design allows for easy adjustment during installation, ensuring a perfect fit for the sink platform.

Total material cost: Less than SG$32

This project demonstrates how a little creativity and basic engineering principles can turn what might be considered waste into a functional, cost-effective solution. I encourage others to look at unused items in their homes with fresh eyes - you might discover their hidden potential!
